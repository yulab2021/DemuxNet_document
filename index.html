Welcome to DemuxNet’s documentation!

DemuxNet is a machine learning-augmented tool for single-cell RNA-seq (scRNA-seq) sample demultiplexing. It automates assignment of cells to samples in pooled runs and predicts missing Cell Multiplexing Oligo (CMO) labels for barcodes with incomplete or ambiguous CMO information.
